SHOWS

New Life | 2022

God's grace and love gives us freedom from the expectations of the world and that of which we hold for ourselves. Join us as we celebrate this new life we have in Him!

I have said these things to you, that in me you may have peace. In the world you will have tribulation. But take heart; I have overcome the world.
- John 16:33


Seasons | 2019

We hope that through our performance, we can share with you our experience of walking with God through the different seasons of life -- through doubts, celebration, pain, joy, sorrow, and everything in between, God is faithful and He loves you so, so deeply.

A time to weep, and a time to laugh; a time to mourn, and a time to dance.
- Ecclesiastes 3:4


New Creation | 2018

New Creation reflects how we have been transformed inwardly by Jesus Christ, so that we may be a light outwardly. Join us as we share through dancing our joy and thanksgiving in Christ.

Therefore, if anyone is in Christ, he is a new creation. The old has passed away; behold, the new has come.
- 2 Corinthians 5:17


Not My Own | 2017

Many of us have felt enslaved to something or other at one point in our lives—an abusive relationship, an addiction, fear, anxiety, hatred. But God saw this and couldn’t bear to see us enslaved any longer. When out of His love, God purchased us with His own life, He made a claim on us and our lives. In gratitude and love, we give back to Him all that He has given us. Tonight, each of our dancer’s prayer to our loving Savior is: “I am Yours, God. I am not my own.”

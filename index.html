Welcome, Class of 2024!

Good to see everyone~ üêØ

Congratulations, Class of 2024! We are SO excited to meet you and dance together in the fall. Founded in 2013, Six14 is Princeton's first Christian dance company, and we are dedicated to sharing the gospel through various forms of dance, including hip-hop, lyrical, contemporary, and praise dance. We'd love to have you join the Six14 family! There is absolutely NO dance experience required to join, just a heart that desires to experience a more intimate relationship with God!
